ABOUT US.

Studio Marani, communication agency based in Milan, has been created in 2011 from Maurizio Marani after his long time experience with the well-known McCann Erickson. Many of his clients, like Illy, L’Espresso Group and Radio Deejay, will follow him in this new adventure and many others such as Radio Capital.

After a fortunate encounter with the copywriter and content manager Anna Sandoval, Studio Marani gains another fundamental member of the creative team. Anna, at that time, was already collaborating with big international brands like Barilla, Volkswagen, Campari, Vodafone, Philips, Kraft, Henkel, San Paolo and so on. In 2013, the Art Director and co-founder of the famous “nonfamous” in 2000, Valentine De Franco, project manager, completes Studio Marani creative team.
